About VGDC

Where ideas become interactive experiences

Connect & Collaborate

Meet passionate developers, artists, designers, and storytellers. Find your perfect team members and bring diverse perspectives to your projects.

Learn & Grow

Access comprehensive resources, tutorials, and mentorship. From Unity basics to advanced programming concepts, we've got you covered.

Create & Showcase

Work on exciting projects, participate in game jams, and showcase your creations. Build a portfolio that opens doors to your future.

Our Mission

To foster a vibrant community of game developers at Stetson University, providing resources, mentorship, and collaborative opportunities for students to explore the exciting world of game development regardless of their experience level.
